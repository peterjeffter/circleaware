OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2025, Circle began with a simple but powerful vision to create a community-driven movement for climate action. What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a collective force for change, uniting people around education, sustainability, and grassroots impact. From local cleanups to youth empowerment and renewable energy projects, our journey is driven by one belief: together, we can build a better future for the planet.
