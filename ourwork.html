OUR WORK

At Circle, we turn passion into action. From climate education to community cleanups and green initiatives, our work is focused on real-world impact. Together, we’re building a future that’s sustainable, just, and powered by collective effort.

Solar for Schools

We installed solar panels at two local schools as part of our mission to promote clean, renewable energy and climate education. This initiative not only reduces the schools' carbon footprint and lowers electricity costs but also serves as a living classroom—giving students a direct look at how solar energy works and why sustainable solutions are essential for the future. By integrating technology with education, we’re helping to shape a generation that’s informed, empowered, and ready to lead the transition to a greener world.
